# Character Bible

## Ethan Voss (protagonist)
- Late 20s. Night-shift radio/audio engineer at a local station.
- Locked visual details: a scar along his left jaw (origin unexplained, a season-long
  mystery thread), and a wristwatch permanently stopped at 3:17 — he wears it anyway.
- Personality: methodical, insomniac, quietly obsessive once he latches onto a
  problem. Distrusts authority instinctively. Dry, deadpan humor as a defense
  mechanism against fear.
- Role: the audience's entry point; the one who starts pulling the thread.

## Maya Reyes
- Late 20s. Locked visual detail: a silver scarf worn constantly, day and night —
  later implied to hide a mark from a prior "noticing" event.
- Personality: sharp, skeptical-turned-believer, the group's researcher — she's the
  one who finds Project Echo in old municipal records.
- Role: turns the mystery from "personal and strange" into "systemic and dangerous."

## Lena
- Late 20s/early 30s. Locked visual detail: a burn scar (placement kept ambiguous in
  early episodes, revealed later as thematically tied to her own encounter).
- Personality: guarded, the most reluctant to get involved, but the most decisive once
  committed. Ex-something (implied institutional background, revealed gradually).
- Role: bridges the group to the idea that they've each been "marked" independently
  before ever meeting.

## Noah
- Age ambiguous, youngest-coded. Locked trait: communicates in short bursts, spends
  most nights on amateur radio scanning dead frequencies.
- Personality: quiet, literal, notices patterns no one else does. Not neurotypical-coded
  explicitly, but written with care — his radio hobby is what breaks the case open in
  Act 2.
- Role: the technical route into "the signal" itself.

## Dr. Adrian Vail (revealed, finale)
- Appears on-screen for the first time in the Episode 24 finale, in the facility's
  exit doorway. Implied architect of Project Echo. Deliberately unexplained until
  Season 2 — do not add backstory beyond "the season proves he's real and he's been
  aware of the group the whole time."

## Locked continuity rules for this cast
- Ethan's watch always reads 3:17, in every scene, every episode, no exceptions.
- Maya's scarf never comes off on-screen in Season 1.
- No dialogue reveals Ethan's scar origin, Lena's burn origin, or Vail's motive this
  season — these are Season 2+ threads.
