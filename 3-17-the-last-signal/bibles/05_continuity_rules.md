# Continuity & Consistency Rules

1. Ethan's watch reads 3:17 in every single shot he appears in, no exceptions.
2. Maya's silver scarf never comes off on camera this season.
3. Lena's burn scar placement, once shown, must not change episode to episode.
4. The 3:17 event is always exactly one second of total stillness — no dialogue, no
   score, no ambient sound during that second, in every episode that features it.
5. No episode may explain: Ethan's scar origin, Lena's burn origin, or Dr. Vail's
   motive — these are intentionally held for Season 2+.
6. "The Watcher" is never shown fully or clearly in Season 1 — glimpses only,
   obscured by reflection distortion, motion blur, or partial framing.
7. Every episode file must restate the character visual anchors from the Visual Bible
   verbatim in its own generation prompts — do not paraphrase them episode to episode.
8. Update this file's paired Master Timeline row immediately after finishing any
   episode's script, before moving to the next episode.
