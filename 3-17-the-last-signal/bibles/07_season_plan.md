# Season 1 Plan

- 24 episodes across 3 acts (see Master Timeline).
- Suggested cadence: 3 episodes/week over 8 weeks (see Channel Launch Kit for the
  exact calendar), each paired with 2-3 Shorts cut-downs for discovery.
- Each episode ships with: full script, generation prompts, audio/edit notes, YouTube
  metadata, thumbnail concept, Shorts cuts, and a continuity-log update.
- Season is designed to end on a cliffhanger (Dr. Adrian Vail's appearance) that
  requires no immediate Season 2 greenlight to feel satisfying — Episode 23 resolves
  the "expose or destroy" choice, so Episode 24's finale beat is a hook, not a
  cliff-hung plot gap.
